Hi, I’m Naimul Hasan, the "Textile Engineer"

I Wanna built

I am a graduate researcher at the Smart Textiles and Nanotechnology Research Group at Colorado State University. Additionally, I work as a graduate teaching assistant in the Department of Design and Merchandising, where I teach the AM220 lab. My expertise lies in the areas of Wearables, Biosensors, Nanotechnology, Electro-spinning, and Composite materials. I earned my bachelor's degree in Textile Engineering from the Khulna University of Engineering & Technology in 2022, where I worked on Triboelectric Nanogenerator.

Currently, I am working closely with Dr. Li on various projects, with a primary focus on developing a human health monitoring system. One of our ongoing projects involves prototyping a colorimetric biosensor, and we are also exploring the use of CNC-based ECG sensors. Additionally, we are researching a drug delivery system utilizing electro-spun nano fibers.

While pursuing these exciting projects, I am keeping my ultimate ambition in mind: to work on space suits. Therefore, I am actively involved in different fields within textile science to broaden my knowledge and skills in preparation for this goal.
